Crafted with Purpose

At LUMÉ, we believe that style shouldn't come at the expense of our planet. Each pair of our eyewear is thoughtfully designed and crafted using sustainable materials like recycled metals, bio-acetate, and responsibly sourced bamboo.

Our commitment extends beyond materials – we plant a tree for every purchase and use plastic-free packaging made from recycled paper products.
